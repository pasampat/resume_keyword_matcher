Software Engineer – Data & Automation

We are seeking a Software Engineer to join our Data & Automation team. The ideal candidate will be passionate about building scalable tools and has a strong background in software development and data analysis.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain automation scripts and data processing pipelines using Python.
- Integrate RESTful APIs and work with SQL databases to store, retrieve, and analyze data.
- Collaborate with data scientists and business analysts to design robust ETL workflows.
- Participate in code reviews, agile ceremonies, and continuous integration/deployment.
- Optimize system performance and troubleshoot issues as they arise.

Requirements:
- Proficiency in Python, SQL, and Git.
- Experience with REST APIs, Pandas, and cloud platforms (AWS preferred).
- Strong understanding of data structures, algorithms, and problem-solving.
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Docker or other containerization tools.
- Familiarity with Spark or big data frameworks.
- Exposure to machine learning or statistical analysis.

If you are eager to work in a fast-paced environment and want to make an impact with innovative data solutions, we would love to hear from you!
